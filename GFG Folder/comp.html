Top Fitness and Sports Competitions in India

1. Mumbai Marathon

The Mumbai Marathon is an annual marathon held in Mumbai, typically in January. It features various categories, including a full marathon, half marathon, and a 5K run.

2. IPL (Indian Premier League)

The Indian Premier League is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India, typically held between March and May. It features teams representing different cities.

3. Pro Kabaddi League

The Pro Kabaddi League is a men's professional Kabaddi league in India, which features teams from various regions, held every year since 2014.

4. Federation Cup (Athletics)

The Federation Cup is an annual athletics competition held in India, featuring various track and field events. It is a key event for athletes to qualify for national competitions.

5. National Games of India

The National Games are multi-sport events held every two years in India. It brings together athletes from various states to compete across a range of sports.

6. Chennai Open

The Chennai Open is a professional tennis tournament held annually in Chennai. It is part of the ATP Tour and attracts many international players.

7. Delhi Half Marathon

The Delhi Half Marathon is an annual road race held in Delhi, typically in November. It includes various distances, promoting fitness and health awareness.

8. Ironman India

The Ironman India is a triathlon competition featuring a 3.8 km swim, 180 km bike ride, and a 42.2 km marathon. It tests the limits of endurance.

9. Khiladi of the Year

Khiladi of the Year is a unique sports competition featuring various challenges and games across different sports disciplines in India.

10. Pogo Amazing Kids Awards

The Pogo Amazing Kids Awards celebrate children excelling in various sports, recognizing their achievements and promoting youth sports participation.

11. All India University Games

The All India University Games are organized for university-level athletes across various sports, promoting competition and excellence among students.

12. National Youth Games

The National Youth Games are held to promote sports among youth in India, providing a platform for young athletes to showcase their talents.

13. Surjit Hockey Tournament

The Surjit Hockey Tournament is an annual hockey competition held in India, featuring various teams competing for the championship.

14. Inter-School Sports Competitions

These competitions are organized annually at various levels for school students, promoting sportsmanship and physical fitness.

15. Tata Mumbai Marathon

The Tata Mumbai Marathon is one of the largest marathons in India, attracting thousands of participants every year and promoting health and fitness.
