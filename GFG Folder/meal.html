Meal Plans

Weight Gain ▾

About Weight Gain

A weight gain diet focuses on consuming a calorie surplus with nutrient-dense foods like nuts, seeds, dairy, and lean proteins. It helps build muscle and increase body mass.

Daily Composition:

  • Carbohydrates: 55-60% of total calories
  • Proteins: 15-20% of total calories
  • Fats: 25-30% of total calories
  • Minerals: Focus on Calcium, Iron, and Magnesium
  • Vitamins: A, D, E, and B-complex vitamins
  • Hydration: 2-3 liters of water daily

Weight Loss ▾

About Weight Loss

A weight loss diet emphasizes calorie control, healthy eating, and portion management, focusing on foods such as vegetables, fruits, lean proteins, and whole grains.

Daily Composition:

  • Carbohydrates: 45-50% of total calories
  • Proteins: 25-30% of total calories
  • Fats: 20-25% of total calories
  • Minerals: Emphasis on Potassium, Calcium, and Iron
  • Vitamins: A, C, D, and B-complex vitamins
  • Hydration: 2-3 liters of water daily
