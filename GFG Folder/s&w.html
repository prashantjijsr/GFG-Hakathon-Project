Safety and Well-being in Fitness & Sports

Stay Safe, Stay Healthy!

Do's

  • Warm up before starting any physical activity to avoid injury.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water before, during, and after workouts.
  • Wear proper gear, including protective equipment, shoes, and clothing.
  • Maintain a balanced diet with nutrients that fuel your body for performance.
  • Listen to your body and take breaks when needed to avoid over-exertion.
  • Cool down with stretches after physical activities to help muscle recovery.
  • Seek advice from professionals, like trainers or coaches, to ensure proper form and technique.

Don'ts

  • Don’t ignore pain—stop exercising immediately if you feel any discomfort.
  • Don’t skip rest days. Your muscles need time to recover and grow stronger.
  • Don’t overeat unhealthy food, especially before or after workouts.
  • Don’t rush into intense workouts without proper training or guidance.
  • Don’t push your body beyond its limits—set realistic goals and pace yourself.
  • Don’t exercise in extreme weather conditions (too hot or too cold) without proper precautions.
  • Don’t neglect mental well-being—fitness is as much about mental health as it is physical.
